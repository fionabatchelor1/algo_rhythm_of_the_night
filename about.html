About us

We are a not for profit organisation, aiming to give easy-to-follow advice and reassurance that you have the power and ability to make a difference.

We passionately believe that if we pay attention to the small steps and changes we can make in our day-to-day lives, and frame our climate conversations around positive actions, we can breathe new life into the movement, and encourage ourselves to keep standing up for what we believe in.

The first step to taking control of our actions to reduce our carbon footprint is to educate ourselves, therefore we aim to provide advice and information based on hands-on tests, expert opinions, and first-hand customer feedback.
